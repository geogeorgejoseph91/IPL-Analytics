Did you know toss winners choose to field over 60% of the time in IPL?

I just completed my first Power BI project: an IPL Cricket Analytics Dashboard spanning 15 seasons, 950 matches, and 296,000 runs.

But the real win wasn't the dashboard. It was what the data revealed:

🔍 **Mumbai Indians** dominate — most titles, highest win rate. Not even close.
🔍 **Batting second wins more.** The numbers don't lie — teams winning the toss pick field, and it works.
🔍 **Home advantage is real.** The crowd factor shows up clearly in the data.
🔍 **2017 belonged to Warner & Kumar** — 641 runs and 26 wickets respectively.

───────────────────

3 interactive report pages. Full drill-through. Slicers by team, season, and player.

Built with Power BI, DAX, and Power Query — from raw data to live dashboard.
